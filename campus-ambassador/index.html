ABOUT US

E-Cell IITM is a student run non-profit organization that aims at fostering innovation and entrepreneurship development and thereby creating a holistic platform for the support of techno-business entrepreneurship by sowing the entrepreneurial seed within the youth of the country.

In sync with motto of E-Cell, IIT Madras, we are reaching out to various colleges in Chennai to nurture the entrepreneurial spirit of young people and to give access to various colleges across Chennai to the opportunities available in IITM; opportunities being in the form of workshops, lectures, competitions, mentors etc.

Responsibilities

Your job as a Campus Ambassador

Publicity

Handling the public image of E-Cell and, spreading information of E-Cell through various activities.

Social Media

Forwarding the emails and sharing e-posters on facebook and whatsapp. Being the face of E-Cell IIT Madras in your college. Updating photos on your Facebook profile.

Promote

Selecting interested and enthusiastic group of people from your college to attend various events conducted by E-Cell throughout the year to promote entrepreneurship.

Participate

Cooperating with CAM (Campus Ambassador Manager) to achieve various targets set by the Head.
