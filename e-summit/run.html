Run the NGO

The event is for all the case study freaks and all those who want to try their hands on social entrepreneurship.

The event format is as follows:

a. Round I : A detailed real life problem that a NGO is facing will be put up for the registered participants online and they will be given enough data and background to come up with a detailed solution. The solution will be judged by the NGO itself based on the feasibility and the relevance to their current situation and top 6-8 teams will be invited for the second round.
b. Round II : This round will be an on-campus presentation round that will be held post a detailed discussion between finalist and the NGO about the proposed solution and how it can moulded to suit their needs. The finalist will pitch this to the jury of the NGO itself.

The winners will be announced later in the day after careful deliberation by the NGO.

Timeline :

1. Release of PS - 5 weeks prior to E-Summit
2. Deadline for submission of a detailed solution of the problem proposed. - 3 weeks prior to E-Summit
3. Announcement of selected teams after filtering - 2 weeks prior to E-summit
4. Session with the concerned NGO (on-campus round) - Day 2
5. Finals - Day 3
