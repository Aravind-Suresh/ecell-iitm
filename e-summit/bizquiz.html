BizQuiz

A competitive, startup knowledge checker yet fun event. One of the most crowd puller event of E-Summit. The event is a must in check-list of every quizzer awaiting for the glory.

We had conducted this quiz in past with Navin Jaykumar as our Quiz Master. BizQuiz is platform where teams compete to showcase their startup world's knowledge to create a mark as Biz-Quizzers.

Event was a huge success and motivated a lot of young minds to venture in a new field of interest. Teams from school and college participated and had graced the stage with their significant knowledge content.

We are planning on having similar yet more competitive and more enjoyable BizQuiz in upcoming Summit.
