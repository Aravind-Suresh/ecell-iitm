Unconference

With startups growing out of each and every corner of the country, students are not far behind in the scene. A lot of students enter this space to solve a problem but with the lack in experience and fierce competition out there, the ideas or to say a potential multi-national company gets lost. We have conceptualised an unconference for those who want to try their sharp, problem-solving minds to solve some of the most complicated ideas.

The conference will include a series of workshops and focused talks on relevant topics to set a framework for them to take up any problem and solve with the correct approach. The workshop will cover the fundamentals needed to achieve this while the talks will give the application and motivation to achieve it.

The students are expected to solve a problem that they get connected to through the experience gained in the conference. The conference aims to create future change-makers and leaders.
