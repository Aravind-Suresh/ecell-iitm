Case Study

Case Study competitions are generally one of the most appreciated and welcomed competition. The competition generally receives a lot of exceptionally insightful entries giving the judge hard time to pick the top teams.

The competition is generally conducted in multiple stages. With the initial round being online for a larger audience to try their hands on solving some real life problem, the competition only gets tougher. The final round is an on-campus presentation of the solution proposed to the judges.

In the past, we have been able to rob in real life intriguing problems from companies like C-Bazaar who were impressed with the entries to graciously provide the top teams for an internship at their company.
