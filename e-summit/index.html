FAQs

>> What is the registration fees for E-Summit’17?
If you are not an IIT Madras student, the registration fees to attend E-Summit’17 is 500 INR only.

If you are an IIT Madras student, there is no registration fees. Please carry your ID card at all the time during E-Summit.

Delegate – Anyone part of the contingent from a college or E-Cell Campus Ambassador is a Delegate. The contingent list from your college (if it is in our network) is present with us. Having said, only if your faculty from college has notified you about this contingency and we receive the details about you, you fall in the category of Delegates. The registration fees for a Delegate is 300 INR only.

The registration fee includes the following:
1. Access to the 3 certified professional Workshops conducted during E-Summit.
2. Access to the complete Lecture series during E Summit.
3. Access to attend Panel Discussions and take part in Business Quiz.
4. Access to the Entertainment Show to be held on 8th April,2017.

>> How do I pay the registration fees?
Registration fees need to be paid on your arrival at the Registration Desk during E-Summit’17 via debit/credit card only.

>> Will I get a certificate for attending E-Summit’17?
Participating in a workshop yields you a certificate. There are three workshops, so you have a chance of getting 3 certificates. These certificates will have the logo of IIT Madras and the company conducting the workshop.

>> Do I need to pay extra fees for workshop/entertainment show/Business Quiz when I have paid the registration fees?
No, your registration fees allows you to attend or participate in any event during E-Summit’17.

>> Where is the Registration Desk set up in E-Summit’17?
Registration Desk will be set up in the lobby of IC&SR Building(200 meters from Gajendra Circle).

>> What time does the registration starts?
The registration starts at following time during E-Summit’17
Day 1, 7th April’17: 4:30PM
Day 2, 8th April’17: 9:00AM
Day 3, 9th April’17: 9:00AM

>> How do I reach IC&SR Building from IIT Madras Main Gate?
You can take the institute bus from IIT Madras Main Gate which runs at a frequency of 15mins and drop off at the IC&SR bus stop.

>> Will Accommodation and food be provided?
Yes, we will provide you with these facilities in IIT Madras campus during E-Summit’17. Accommodation and food charges are not included in the registration fee.
Accommodation will be provided at the rate of 200 INR per person per day, with an extra 300 INR as security deposit with respect to availability of rooms. The allotment will be on first come first serve basis for people coming from outside of Chennai while people coming from Chennai will be less priority for accommodation.
