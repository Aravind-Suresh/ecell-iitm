PITCHFEST

Pitchfest is a unique initiative by IITM to fund some of the most promising startups in the country. Start-ups from across the country can pitch their ideas and products in front of an elite panel of investors and C-level executives. The winners can hope to get a generous seed funding from the investors.

Who is it for?

    The competition caters to startups in the following phases:
  • Young start-ups struggling with initial funds- usually student startups from educational institutes who might be looking for mentoring, funding or incubation.The startups in their ideation stage or early stage are preferred for this category.
  • Operational start-ups â€“ who might need funding to get started.These start-ups should be well past the ideation stage.

What is at stake?

  • The winners will get invaluable mentoring and generous funding from our esteemed judging panel.
  • The Incubation Cell of IIT Madras may incubate some early stage start-ups.
  • Prize money of 75,000 INR will be rewarded to the best team by IITM E-Cell.
