Registrations and Screening of teams



  • Start-ups and teams interested in participating need to fill the registration form before the mentioned deadline.

  • Each team is required to submit an executive summary along with the form. The guidelines for writing the executive summary can be found under the 'Executive Summary' tab. The summary should not exceed two pages.

  • The teams need to submit the drive link to the executive summary (in pdf format) along with the registration form.

  • The screening of received applications will be done based on the executive summary.

    • Note that applications sent without the executive summary drive link will not be considered for screening.
