General guidelines for the executive Summary



  • Submitting the executive summary is mandatory for all teams registering for Pitchfest
  • The write up should not exceed two pages.
  • The drive link to the summary in pdf format should be pasted in the registration form under the relevant field.
  • The executive summary should adequately project each of the aspects mentioned below. These will be considered while screening the applications received.
  • The early stage start-ups may not be in a position to elaborate on some of the mentioned points (expansion strategy and business model, market analysis, financial arrangements). These points will not be considered for screening as far as young start-ups are concerned, however we strongly recommend that you attempt all the addressed points.

  • About the start-up

    • The chief objective/vision
    • Product description
    • Market for the product

  • Journey up to now in a nutshell

    • Identification of the problem statement
    • Journey through the ideation stage.
    • Problems faced, solutions deployed, crucial decisions made.
    • Did you receive any help/ incubation?
    • All the milestones achieved till today.

  • About the product

    • Uniqueness
    • Market analysis of your product
    • Competitive advantage and sustainability of your product

  • Growth plan

    • Long term and short term goals
    • Expansion Strategy and business model.
    • Financial arrangements and revenue generation

  • How important is this seed funding for your start-up and how would you utilise it?


  • Potential investor returns and payback plan
