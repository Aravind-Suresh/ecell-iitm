The competition

The Mock Pitching Session (08/04/17)

The shortlisted teams will be guided a day prior to the formal event by our experts to help them pitch in front of the jury the following day.

Note that this is NOT an eliminatory round.

The Final Pitching Round (09/04/17)

  • The Pitching sessions for both categories will happen in parallel.
  • Each of the teams will be given a maximum of 5 minutes to pitch in their product, business model, investor return potential.
  • The teams will be allowed to make use of presentations, or video, or use any other media to impress the esteemed jury.
  • Following the presentation, the investors will be allowed to question the team on various points as they may desire. This session will last for about 5 minutes.
